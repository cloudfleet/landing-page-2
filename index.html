All-in-One Cloud Solution

We believe that everyone has a right to data privacy!

We value having this privilege – The cloudfleet blimp is a device that enables you to communicate securely via email, manages your files, calendar and contacts. It is easy to use, keeps your personal data safe and secure and will grow into a smart personal assistant.

Our Features

Choice is a beautiful thing. You can have both security and privacy!

Self-Hosted Email

A modern and user-friendly web mail service, with the reassurance that only you have access to your email.

Store. Sync & Share

Upload your files directly into the cloudfleet blimp from anywhere and keep multiple devices in sync.

Calendar, Contacts & Notes

Universal access to your own portable office to help you stay efficient and organised.

Encrypted & Secure

We have devoted time & effort implementing well-trusted encryption into our infrastructure to provide you with powerful protection to your data.

Simple, Intuitive, Easy to Use

With a lightweight user interface, that has all the widgets needed to give you quick access to your emails, storage and device usage, cloudfleet gives you the ease of logging into your account using a web browser on any computer or smartphone.

We have an intelligent dashboard that enables you to create accounts for other people, as well as an integrated user-friendly timeline which seamlessly gives you information on your past interactions and much more...
